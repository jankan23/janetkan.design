Ugly Cider

Ugly craft cider uses unselected ‘ugly’ supermarket fruits to produce a refreshing summertime beverage. The supermarket theme extends to the 4-pack packaging, which reflects the use of supermarket fruit crates and tagging to deliver the apples. The brand look and feel is unique to the craft cider category where a lot of brands carry a traditional look. The hand-generated logo places emphasis on the human contribution to the definition of ‘ugly’.
